Governance

Trust governance and public records

The Academy Trust (tAcT) was set up in August 2014 as a registered public charitable body under the aegis of the Indian Academy of Sciences, Bengaluru.

The current activity report describes the trust’s objectives, current trustees, outreach committee, and programme record for April 2022 to March 2025. The sections below bring those details into one public page for reference.

Current report in use Activity Report APRIL 2022-MARCH 2025
Core objectives listed in the report Science education outreach, academia-industry partnership, and endowment chair professorship.
Committee model The outreach committee is composed of Trustees, Fellows, and Associates for a two-year term, extendable for a second term.
