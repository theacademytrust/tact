Springer-Nature Chair

How the chair is tied to the Academy

The Springer-Nature Chair is tied to the Academy's journal publishing agreement with Springer-Nature.

What the chair does

The program features an eminent scientist who visits India as the Springer-Nature Chair and delivers lectures and holds interactions with scientists in scientific institutions across India related to their area of expertise.

Programme Shape

Chair appointment

An eminent scientist is hosted as the Springer-Nature Chair.

Lectures

The visitor delivers lectures in scientific institutions across India.

Interactions

The visit includes interactions with scientists related to the Chair's area of expertise.

Context in tAcT's Objectives

Trust objective

The activity report states that tAcT focuses on capacity building, promoting outreach activities where intervention is urgently needed, fostering innovation and excellence in Science and Technology, collaborating with other organizations for joint programmes, improving research and education in science and engineering, and setting up science education chair/professorships.

Three documented modes of work

The report lists Science Education Outreach, Academia-Industry Partnership, and Endowment Chair Professorship as the ways through which the Trust fulfils these objectives.
