29th April 2023: Conducted by Academy Fellow Prof. KN Ganeshaiah on 29th April 2023 at National College Basavanagudi, where he illustrated the importance of curiosity and rigorous scientific study with demos and several real-life examples, including one about a student of Jane Goodall whose observation of chimpanzees led to a whole new field of research called Zoopharmacognosy; and one about his own PhD student, whose study of the nesting behaviour of birds showed how much intelligence lies behind innate natural instincts. The interactive session that followed was filled with questions and ideas covering almost all fields of science.
