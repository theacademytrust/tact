3rd March 2023 - Indian Academy of Sciences

Organised in collaboration with the Academy and Science Utsav, this full-day event was designed as a practical guide that science teachers could use on a regular basis. Science communicator Manasa Chidambar engaged the participants in craftwork and other activities that they could implement in their classrooms.

The next session was conducted by veteran Teacher Trainer and Principal of National College Jayanagar, Dr. YC Kamala. She shared ideas and case studies where students not only conducted methodical research to fulfil project goals, but also learned presentation skills to effectively communicate their work.

Shashank Karnam showed how simple materials can be used to demonstrate scientific phenomena and concepts found in school textbooks. Apart from these, there was a session on hands-on tinkering in Electronics & Coding. At the end of the event, mementos, and books published by the Academy were presented to the resource persons and participants.
