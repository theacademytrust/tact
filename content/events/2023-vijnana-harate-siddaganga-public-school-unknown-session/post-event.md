16th November 2023: Prof. Vikram Jayaram, who conducted the harate for high school students of Siddaganga Public School, started with Lord Rayleigh's century-old experiment and explained how that led to the invention of detergents used for washing clothes. From there, he went on to connect the dots showing how advances in materials research and engineering made it affordable and safe for people to travel by flight; how durable and smart materials find uses in everything from heavy industry to clinical surgery; and so on. From questions about familiar household objects (How can a kitchen knife that cuts anything, break?) to geopolitics (How China has a stranglehold over the world because of its rare earth resources), the harate addressed diverse topics.
