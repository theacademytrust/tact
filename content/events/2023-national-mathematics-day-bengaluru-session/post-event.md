22 December 2023 - Acharya Patshala College

tAcT also collaborated with the Ramanujan Mathematics Club and the Department of Mathematics of Acharya Patshala College to celebrate National Mathematics Day 2023. More than 160 participants from different colleges attended the event.

D. Radha Krishna, Retired Professor and Vice -Principal of Vijaya college, Bangalore inaugurated the morning session and gave a brief insight into the life history and achievements of the Srinivasa Ramanujan.

N.S. Kumar, Educationist & Researcher engaged the students on the topic "Fun with Maths Counting & Computing", where he explained about Ramanujan numbers, Magic Squares, Determinant concepts etc., in a very simple manner.

T.K. Prasanna Murthy, Karnataka State Resource person for Secondary school Mathematics, talked about the works of Ramanujan and infinite series of numbers, continued fraction, Pell's equation, and Brahmagupta's formula. He demonstrated various models related to Mathematics to help understand the concepts in a very easy manner.

Certificates were distributed to the winners of Maths Talent Search Examination conducted for PU students on 13th Dec 2023, during the program.
