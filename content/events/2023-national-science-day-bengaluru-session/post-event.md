28th February 2023 - Bangalore City University

Organised in collaboration with the Academy, National Science Day was celebrated at Bangalore University Central College campus, where Sir CV Raman made the official announcement of his historic discovery. Prof. Lingaraja Gandhi, Vice-Chancellor of the University, enlightened the audience of this fact, and a surprising number of other connections that the Nobel Laureate had with the University. A film showcasing the life of the great scientist was shown, along with presentations by Prof. Utpal Nath of IISc who spoke on how genes regulate growth in plants, and Prof. GC Anupama of IIA who took the audience on an awe-inspiring journey of the Cosmos.
