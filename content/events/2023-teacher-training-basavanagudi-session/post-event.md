2nd November 2023 - BITS Pilani Goa

As part of the Joint Science Academies' Education Panel (SEP) Mini-Symposium for invited teachers held during the Academy's 89th Annual Meeting at BITS Pilani - K K Birla Goa Campus, tAcT invited veteran Teacher Trainer and Principal of National College Basavanagudi, Dr. YC Kamala to conduct a session on pedagogy. Her presentation included practical tips on engaging high school and college students via activities involving not just observation and experiments, but how get them to write research papers and make presentations professionally.
