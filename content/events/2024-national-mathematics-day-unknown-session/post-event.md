24 December 2024. More than 100 participants from different colleges attended the event which sought to enhance students' mathematical reasoning and problem-solving abilities, with the long-tern view of inspiring students to pursue careers in mathematical sciences and research.

Krishna Chaitanya, well-known math communicator, conducted a session on "Unlocking the Wonders of the Essence of Math," following it up with a Mathematics quiz.

Prof. Ramesh BK of Jain College conducted the afternoon session with a discussion on "Numbers Beyond Counting: The Magic of Number Theory," covering prime numbers and their cryptographic applications.
