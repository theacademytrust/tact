6-8 January 2023: tAcT participated in Science in Action, Bengaluru's biggest science fest, held at the BVJ Science Centre between 6-8 January 2023, featuring exhibitions, demonstrations and activities by organisations like ISRO, FSSAI, JN Planetarium, Agastya Foundation, etc. In a joint effort with the Academy, tAcT held an exhibition and 2 harate programs.

The first was conducted on Saturday by Shashi Thutupalli of NCBS, who engaged high school students, teachers and the general public in a freewheeling session that explored the most fundamental questions of nature, and emerged at the other end with the realisation that most "answers" are simply the beginning of other questions!

On Sunday, Uday Maitra of IISc, with the assistance of his wife Samita, held an audience hooked with demos on various chemical phenomena. With a colourful mixture of fun and fact, he showed how "Chemistry is everywhere!"
