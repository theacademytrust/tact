28th February every year to commemorate the discovery of the Raman Effect. This year's event was celebrated by the Academy and tAcT, in association with Karnataka Jnana Vijnana Samiti Bengaluru Chapter. The full-day event featured 3 Vijnana Harate sessions for high school students of Bapu High School. The first was conducted by VSS Sastry and Praveen, who engaged the students in activities like origami to explain mathematical concepts.

The second harate was conducted by Vikram Jayaram on how the "common" materials that are used by society on an everyday basis are the result of decades of research and development in Materials Engineering research. In the afternoon, Kartik Sarkar, astrophysicist from RRI, discussed astronomical phenomena in a highly interactive session with the students.
