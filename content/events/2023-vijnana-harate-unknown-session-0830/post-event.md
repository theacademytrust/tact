30th August 2023: Organised in collaboration with KSCST, for high school students of Aditi Public school the day featured demonstration of the Scientific Method by veteran science communicator Madhusudhan of JN Planetarium, followed by a presentation and experiment-observe-infer activity conducted by a team of creative professionals from Navakshitija.

These were followed by a visit to the Raman Museum and RRI Archives Gallery.
