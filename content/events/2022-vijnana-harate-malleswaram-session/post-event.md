8th July 2022: The full-day program was a collaboration between the Academy Trust, Indian Academy of Sciences and Raman Research Institute. About 80 students from Bapu Composite Pre-University College, Yeshwanthpur, and Govt. High School, Malleswaram, participated along with their science teachers.

The morning sessions included a presentation on 'Atmospheric composition and climate change' by Academy Associate Binita Pathak, 'Perspectives in Math and Art' by Academy Fellow Supurna Sinha, and experiments on Electricity & Magnetism by Gopalakrishna and Biswal from Visveswaraiah Industrial Museum.

The post-lunch session was conducted by Madhusudan from the Jawaharlal Nehru Planetarium, followed by a visit to the Archives Gallery and the Raman Museum.
