4-6th November 2022: 3 days of programs were conducted by tAcT during the Academy's 88th Annual Meeting at SRM University, AP.

The first day's program was conducted by Madhusudan of Jawaharlal Nehru Planetarium, Bengaluru, whose experiments in optics, thermodynamics and sound for students of Sri Charan Vidyaniketan and SRM University.

On the second day, Academy Fellow Prof. Debabrata Dash of BHU Varanasi held a session for students of Shamrock High School on research in medicine.

The third day's session was a harate on Astronomy & Astrophysics by Academy Fellow Prof. Ram Sagar of IIA Bengaluru, for students of Lakireddy Balireddy Engineering College.
