26th Aug 2024: In the first joint program with JNCASR, students of Vanita Sadan, a school for economically deprived children in Mysuru, spent a curiosity-fuelled day in the JNCASR campus and labs guided by Vinayak Pattar. Apart from participating in science demos and experiments, the students listened to talks, and interacted with, Ranjani Viswanatha and Shwetha Shivaprasad. An immediate and encouraging aftereffect of the day's program was, when asked for their feedback, many of the girl students responded that they would like to pursue a career in scientific research.
