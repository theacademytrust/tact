13-27th April 2023: The first Vijnana Aranya (remote and rural science outreach) program was organised in collaboration with the Centre for Applied Sciences, Government Degree College (Men), Andhra Pradesh. With the assistance of M. Pradeep and other faculty members, Prof. HS Misra of GITAM University, Visakhapatnam, conducted four full-day workshops on various topics related to Life Sciences and Ecology for students of Government College (Men)-Srikakulam (13th April 2023), Government College (Women)-Srikakulam (15th April 2023), Gurajada PU College (20th April 2023) and Sri Aditya College (27th April 2023).
