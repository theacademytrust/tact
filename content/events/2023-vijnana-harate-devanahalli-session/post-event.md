14th July 2023: Conducted by Dr. Seetha Somasundaram, Program Director of ISRO's Space Science Program Office during the Mars Orbiter Mission, for high school students of Anantha Vidyanikethan, Devanahalli. Although the harate started with Astronomy, it eventually led to a Q&A session covering subjects ranging as far afield as theoretical mathematics and applied engineering.

This was followed by a visit to the RRI Archives Gallery and the Raman museum.
