8th Nov 2024: Students from five regional schools attended a full-day program organised jointly by tAcT and the outreach team of NISER Bhubaneswar, held during the Academy's 90th Annual Meeting. Vijnana harate sessions were conducted by Prof Sankara Rao of IISc who revealed astonishing facts about the history and diversity of trees in India, and by Prof Ranganath Navalgund of ISRO who engaged the youngsters in an impromptu discussion on how satellite technology is invaluable not only in predicting meteorological phenomena but in helping to maintain fishing communities along the Indian coastline. The students also attended demo sessions at various labs of the institute, led by Dr. Debasmita Pankaj Alone and the NISER outreach team.
