1st March 2025: Extending the National Science Day program into a remote outreach, tAcT organised the third Vijnana Aranya at Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Residential School, Doddakaanya village near Nanjangud. More than a hundred students from 6th to 9th Grade participated in the full-day program. The morning session was conducted by popular science educator 'Pelican' Manu who delivered a talk on ecology and the environment, followed by a demonstration on organic farming. The students then formed small groups to create 'botanical art' with leaves, twigs and stones found within the grounds of the campus. In the afternoon, popular arachnologist Dr. Abhijit APC of Mysuru astonished the audience with secrets about the world of common household spiders, and how different species play a vital role in maintaining the ecological balance.
