9th September 2022: About 35 science teachers from various schools in and around Bengaluru participated in the full-day science and pedagogy workshop which was facilitated by our collaborators ScienceUtsav and Seed2Sapling Education. Sessions were conducted by the S2S team and Shashank of ScienceUtsav.

Prof. Abdul Rehman Pasha, distinguished scholar and educator, made a presentation on Young Minds and Scientific Temper: The Role of Teachers.

The post-lunch session featured discussions on pedagogy, followed by a visit to the Raman Museum.
