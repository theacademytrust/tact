21st August 2023: Students from 2 institutions, SBS PU College and Vishwa Vidyapeeth, participated in a full-day program that featured presentations by two eminent speakers. Prof. Jayarama Kadandale, Director of Centre for Human Genetics, explained techniques like Karyotyping to identify chromosomal abnormalities, and how efforts are being made to educate people about the genetic basis of birth defects, so that these conditions could be prevented from being passed on to future generations. Dr. Yelamaggad from the Centre for Nano and Soft Matter Sciences (CeNS) created a buzz with his talk, "How one can become invisible!" and showed ways in which it could be achieved through research into exotic materials.

The harates were followed by a visit to the Raman Museum and RRI Archives Gallery.
