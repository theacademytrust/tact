13th Sep 2024: The first program was conducted at MC Thalalu Govt School, Saragur, where primary students participated in science demos and a puppet show on ecology by award-winning science educator Subbulakshmi. The afternoon session, conducted by professional apiculturist Guruprasad, was attended by high school students as well as interested villagers who wanted to learn about beekeeping as a supplementary means of income. An exhibition of science projects by the students of the school was also on display for the occasion.
