9th September 2023: Dr. S. Seetha, former Director, Space Science Program, ISRO, conducted two harates at two venues in Mysuru. The first was at Vanitha Sadana, a school for girls from underprivileged communities, where she answered questions from curious kids and elders about astronomy in general, and the Aditya (Solar) Mission in particular. The second harate was arranged by Kollegala Sharma of Kutuhali at a Social Welfare Department hostel for young women, where she shared instances and advice from both her professional and personal life with the students.
