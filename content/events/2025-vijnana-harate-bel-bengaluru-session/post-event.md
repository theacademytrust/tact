3rd January 2025: The Academy and tAcT, in association with Karnataka Jnana Vijnana Samiti Bengaluru Chapter, organised a Vijnana Harate for high school students of BEL Public School. Conducted by veteran maths and science educator VSS Sastry, the students learned mathematical concepts by doing origami, and about the scale and scope of the Solar System using props spread across the RRI campus. The students also visited the Raman Museum and RRI Archives Gallery.
