29th November 2024: The Academy and tAcT hosted the 2024 Young Kalam Science Fest organised by HCL Foundation in the Academy auditorium and RRI Library and campus. School students and teachers from several Government primary schools from across Karnataka participated in the event which featured a science model exhibition that was judged by scientists from RRI and senior teachers and educators. The prize distribution ceremony was followed by a discussion on how science can be made more accessible and interesting to schoolchildren through simple and innovative methods.
