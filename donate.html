Receipt and Tax Documentation

Donations are eligible for tax exemption under Sections 80G and 12A of the Income Tax Act, subject to prevailing rules and the details submitted by the donor.

  • Donor name exactly as it should appear on the receipt.
  • PAN, postal address, email, and contact number.
  • Amount, transfer date, and transaction reference.
  • Purpose note, if the gift is intended for a specific programme or fellowship.
Send receipt details to trust@ias.ac.in

Other Ways to Give

Cheques or Demand Drafts should be made in favour of The Academy Trust, payable at Bengaluru.

Mail them to the Executive Secretary, The Academy Trust, Indian Academy of Sciences, C. V. Raman Avenue, Bengaluru. Include contact details for acknowledgement and receipt processing.

Institutional Support

Institutions and CSR teams can write to the Trust office to discuss support for specific outreach programmes, fellowships, or long-term corpus needs.
