All donations are eligible for tax exemption under Sections 80G and 12A of the Income Tax Act, subject to prevailing rules. Please share your PAN and address when you make a transfer so that we can issue a receipt.

For receipts After making a transfer, email the transaction reference, donor name, PAN, postal address, and contact number to trust@ias.ac.in.

How to make a donation

The Academy Trust currently accepts donations through bank transfer, cheque, Demand Draft, and institutional support routes.

  • Online bank transfer to the account listed above.
  • Cheque or Demand Draft in favour of "The Academy Trust", payable at Bengaluru.
  • Institutional or CSR support for specific outreach programmes or fellowships.

Cheques / DDs may be mailed to the Executive Secretary, The Academy Trust, Indian Academy of Sciences, Bengaluru. Please include your contact details to help us acknowledge the support.

Receipt and tax documentation

To help the office issue the receipt correctly, share the following details after the donation is made.

  • Donor name exactly as it should appear on the receipt.
  • PAN, postal address, email, and contact number.
  • Amount, transfer date, and transaction reference.
  • Purpose note, if the gift is intended for a specific programme or fellowship.

Receipt and exemption documentation are issued subject to prevailing statutory requirements and the details submitted by the donor.

How your contribution is used

  • Small-group visits where students meet scientists and work with simple activities.
  • Travel, materials, and coordination for outreach in rural, tribal, and urban schools.
  • Support for fellowships and the Chair Professorship associated with tAcT.
  • Documentation and materials that teachers can continue using after the visit.
