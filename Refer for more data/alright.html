Documents & Governance

Key trust documentation (transparency & compliance)

The following documents and information are recommended to be made available on the TAcT website for transparency and public record:

  • Name of the Trust
    The Academy Trust (tAcT)
  • Registration number & date of approval
    Registration details (e.g. Registered on 19 August 2014). PDF of registration certificate and date.
  • Founding members & contact particulars
    List of founding members and contact info (Prof. D. Chatterjee, A.K. Sood, J. Srinivasan, Uday Maitra, R. Varadarajan, M. R. S. Rao, S. Chandrasekharan, R. Ramaswamy, S. Sivaram, Chandrima Shaha).
  • Current Board of Trustees
    Names, tenure, and appointment dates of current trustees.
  • Audited & certified statement of accounts
    Annual financial statements from 2014-15 onwards (balance sheet, liabilities, assets, investments, advances, cash & bank balances, notes).
  • No-dues letters & tax compliance
    Income tax/IT clearance and any relevant correspondence confirming settlement of taxes.
  • Annual report of TAcT
    Annual reports documenting activities, outreach metrics and programme outcomes.
  • IT exemption approvals (section 35)
    Copies of approvals for tax exemptions under relevant sections (if applicable).
  • FCRA approval
    Foreign Contribution Regulation Act registration number and certificate (if applicable).

We will add PDF links here as the documents are consolidated. Please upload official files to the shared G-Drive and we will link them from this page.
